About Us

Making games for fun and profit!

Spacewalkers is a group of experienced developers working on their debut game inspired by the world created by Polish sci-fi author.

We have decades of experience shared among our team, gained over many projects in well established studios. After creating multitude of games both with publishers and while independent, we decided to pool our resources, know-how and passion to create a gamedev studio that will achieve even more.
